Development of a Device for Estimation of Ambient Temperature by Measuring Ultrasonic Propagation Velocity in Air
Saransh, Nitin Dhiman, P. K. Dubey
International Conference on Ultrasonics and Materials Science for Advanced Technology (ICUMSAT), 2025   (Oral Presentation)
code / video

We present a high-precision, non-contact framework for ambient temperature estimation using ultrasonic propagation velocity in air. We demonstrate robust, real-time, and repeatable measurement performance and introduce a low-cost, reliable approach for accurate temperature sensing in precision measurement applications.
